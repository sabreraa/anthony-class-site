About The Artist

Hello there! My name is Anthony, and I'm from Alexandria, Virginia. I'm a graphic design student currently enrolled at Virginia Commonwealth University.

My love for graphic design started in high school when I discovered two of my interest can be combined: art and technology.

My art style tends to approach minimalism, poster-making, book design, and brand logos.

When I'm not working on graphic design projects, I hang out with my dear friends and play sports.
